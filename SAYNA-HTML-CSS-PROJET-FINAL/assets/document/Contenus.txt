○ [Events]... your gateway to connect with events around your area
○ [Events] works in four simple steps.
○ It's simple, on [Events] homepage, enter the name of the location
you want to spend some good quality time with people and click on
search.
○ Go to check the events nearby the location you searched for and
apply for any [Events] available. Every [gathering] has a subject,
that you can search for too! If no [Events] are available on the
location you selected, you can create and share your event.
○ Select the [Events], wait for the scheduled day and go! Be
prepared to meet new people, create new connections and have a
great time!
○ Once you are at the [Events] location, look for the host, enjoy
and have fun!
○ Every month a new you might find [Events] near you and enjoy the
company with people whom you can share mutual interests
○ You can create [Events] events around your area and share them
with people you know and with whoever wants to connect
○ [Events] let you meet new people, share interests and spreading
love
